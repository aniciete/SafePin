About Us

About SafePin

SafePin is a community-driven safety platform designed to bridge the gap between citizens and authorities. By enabling anonymous reporting and implementing a structured visual access system, we ensure sensitive information reaches the right people while keeping communities informed about verified safety concerns.

How It Works

Report

Anonymously report incidents by taking a picture or placing a pin on our interactive map and providing details about what happened.

Verify

Local authorities review and verify reports to ensure accuracy before they become publicly visible.

Inform

Verified reports appear on the public map, helping community members stay informed about safety concerns.

Act

Community awareness leads to better decision-making, improved responses from authorities, and ultimately safer neighborhoods.
