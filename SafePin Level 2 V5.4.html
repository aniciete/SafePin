SafePin Help Center

How do I report an incident?

To report an incident, navigate to the "Map View" section and drop a pin on the location of the incident. You can then provide details about the crime, hazard, or emergency.

How are reports verified?

Barangay officials (Level 2 users) have access to a dedicated dashboard where they can review and verify submitted reports. This ensures the accuracy and reliability of the data.

Can I report anonymously?

Yes, SafePin V1 is designed to allow residents to anonymously report incidents. Your identity will be protected.

Who can access the data?

Access to incident data is role-based. Barangay officials can only view data relevant to their specific jurisdiction.
